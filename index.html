About the Project

Every week my wife and I try to plan out our meals for the week. This is a compilation of some favorites. We like a wide variety and always try to think about what will make good leftovers.

Weeknight Recipes

Fajitas

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Lime
  • Olive Oil
  • Spice Blend
  • Guacamole
  • Tortillas

Directions

  1. Marinade chicken in lime juice, olive oil & spice blend
  2. Grill chicken
  3. Sautee peppers and onion
  4. Serve with tortillas and guacamole
